Cost Estimation Details :

Seed Cost

The cost of seeds will vary depending on the crop you choose. For example, rice seeds cost around 2000 INR per acre, while soybean seeds cost around 1800 INR per acre.

Fertilizer Cost

Fertilizers are essential for plant growth and can be costly. On average, you can expect to spend around 1500-2500 INR per acre on fertilizers, depending on the crop and soil type.

Irrigation Cost

Irrigation is critical for crop growth, especially during dry seasons. Depending on the irrigation method you choose, you can expect to spend around 4000-7000 INR per acre on irrigation.

Labor Cost

Labor cost is one of the most significant expenses in crop production. On average, you can expect to spend around 8000-15000 INR per acre on labor, depending on the crop and labor availability.

Equipment Cost

The cost of equipment will vary depending on the type and size of the equipment needed. For example, a tractor can cost around 50000 INR, while a combine harvester can cost upwards of 60000 INR.
